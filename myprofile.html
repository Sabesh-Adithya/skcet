My Projects

1) Smart Street Light Automation

Smart street light automation involves using advanced technologies to improve the efficiency, functionality, and sustainability of street lighting systems. These systems typically use sensors, communication networks, and data analytics to adjust lighting based on real-time conditions. For example, lights can automatically dim or brighten based on the time of day, weather, or the presence of pedestrians and vehicles. This not only enhances safety and visibility but also reduces energy consumption and maintenance costs. Additionally, smart street lights can be integrated with other smart city infrastructure to support a range of applications, from monitoring air quality to providing Wi-Fi hotspots.

2) Smart Energy DC Meter

SMart Energy DC Meter is an innovative project designed to monitor and manage direct current (DC) energy usage efficiently. The meter offers real-time tracking of various DC parameters such as voltage, current, and power consumption. Key features include high precision measurements, user-friendly interface for data display, and connectivity options for remote monitoring and control. The project aims to enhance energy efficiency, support sustainable practices, and provide valuable insights for both residential and industrial applications.
